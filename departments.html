Popular department

The following department are available at Ebenezer Hospital

cardiac clinic

Ebenezer Hospital cardiologists constantly seek new knowledge to improve diagnosis and therapies for people with heart disease also provide compassionate care and utilize high quality, advanced technology to accomplish your health goals.

Surgery

The Surgery is covered by highly qualified specialists. We have modern infrastructure and the latest knowledge from science and research. When surgery is needed you will receive the best care before, during and after surgery.

Pediatrics

Our team provides comprehensive evaluation and specialized treatment for babies, children and teens. We work closely with many kinds of healthcare providers to care for patients who have medical or developmental issues.

dental clinic

We provide specialized dental care for adults and children depending on the person's needs and operate as a centre of referral for the local dental community and for patients who need specialist treatment. All dental services are available
